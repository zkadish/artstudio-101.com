We engage our clients to find artwork that achieves their environmental and aesthetic goals.

artstudio 101 is a full service art consulting firm that will help select and specify art for any healthcare, corporate or residential project. We have a dedicated team of consultants who collaborate on every step of a project, from initial conceptualization and budgeting to final installation.

By operating as consultants, rather than a gallery, our team has access to an unparalleled network of galleries, collections, vendors, and both local and national artists who work in every conceivable medium and scale.

To complement the art and further enhance our client’s environment, we provide our clients with the highest standards in affordable custom framing. Our consultants can construct a customized creative solution that will meet any budget, deadline or project parameters.

As the project comes to fruition, we are able to facilitate artwork handling, installation or storage by leveraging our list of highly qualified and recommended licensed professionals.

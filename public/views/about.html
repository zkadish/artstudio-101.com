We are full service art consulting firm working with designers, architects and corporate clients to create dynamic customized spaces.

artstudio 101 is locally owned and operated in Phoenix, Arizona. We have been satisfying art consulting needs both locally and nationally since 2000.

Our philosophy is to “simplify” the process and serve as the single point of contact for all artwork requirements. We offer the highest quality of service; original concepts and design; project management; custom framing; and on-site installation.

Our commitment is to ensure that the artwork perfectly fulfills each client’s vision for the space; is installed on time and on budget; and is characteristic of the client’s program.

artstudio 101 proudly sponsors sustainability and actively participates in meaningful efforts including Go Green, HALO Animal Rescue, and Habitat for Humanity.
